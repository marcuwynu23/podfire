What is PodFire?

You write code; deployment should get out of your way. Link your repository, and PodFire handles the rest—no YAML labyrinths, no server checklists. Built for teams who’d rather ship than configure.

Learn more

Your infrastructure, your rules

Deploy on your servers, your cloud, or your datacenter. Sign in with your existing repository host, link the repositories and branches you use daily, and add agents wherever you need capacity. Scale by adding more agents—no re-architecture. When you self-host, you keep full control and full visibility for you and your team.

Learn more

From push to production, automatically

PodFire detects your stack and runs the right build—no custom pipeline scripting required. Enable auto-deploy and go from commit to live in minutes. Spend less time on DevOps and more time writing code. Perfect for modern workflows and fast iteration.

Learn more

One dashboard: apps, logs, routing, DNS

Manage all your apps, stream logs, and trigger deploys from one place. The built-in edge gateway and optional managed DNS route your domains to the right services—no manual config. Built for developers who want a clear view of what’s running and where.
